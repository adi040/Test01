How to Use the Calculator?

To convert your marks to percentages:

  1. Determine the total marks you scored in a test.
  2. Identify the weightage of the test (e.g., 10%).
  3. Convert the weightage to a decimal (e.g., 10% becomes 10/100 = 0.1).
  4. Multiply your scored marks by the decimal value.
  5. Multiply the result by 4 to get the percentage value.

For example, if you scored 25 marks in Cycle Test 1, which has a total weightage of 10%:

  • 25 marks x 0.1 = 2.5
  • 2.5 x 4 = 10%

* Disclaimer: Marks must be converted to percentages before calculations.
